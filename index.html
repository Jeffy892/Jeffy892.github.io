About me

I have been in AME for 3 years now, I have learned a lot of techniques and other things about technology and media. In this past years it has been a great journey to be in this academy and doing all good and fun things that only AoAME students can do. During my time in AoAME I have learned how to work with different people, learn things about media and information technology and this things has led me to be motivated to pursue my dreams to learn more things that will benefit me in the coming future. In AoAME you are required to work with other people. In the past 2 years I have been working with other people to do our projects together such as filming, creating a script, preparing a pitch and other Group related things. Due to this, I have learned to work well with other people and communicate efficiently than before. Because of AME I really learned how to communicate and work with other people. AoAME also teaches you about Information Technology such as applications and coding. 3 years ago when I was in AoAME 1, I learned how to code HTML, CSS and JQuery because my teacher, Mr. Singer has a assignment to code and finish the lesson in Code Academy. I was so deeply into it that I went ahead than other people and I finished Php, JQuery and started other languages in the Code Academy. I also learned how to use Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Flash, After Effects and Premiere Pro during the time in AME. Because of AoAME I learned a lot of things from coding to using different applications. As being a part of AME family I gained a lot of experience and techniques such as communication to other people, and working with people as well getting familiar with technology. As I leave my high school career I will be carrying this skills and experience that I have earned from being part of this family.
